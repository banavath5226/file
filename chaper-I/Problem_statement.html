1.2Proposed System

  • This system is used to get the sentiment in the given reviews.
  • This system takes the reviews extracted by the user.
  • Here we used RStudio for Visualization.

    Sentimental analysis occurs in 3 steps

    1. Evaluative terms expressing opinions must be extracted from the review.
    2. The polarity, of the opinions must be determined.
    3. Finally, the review is classified with respect to sentiment classes, such as Positive and Negative, based on the polarity of the opinions it contains
